Agriculture Calculator

Agri Calculator or Digital System of Plants Problem Identification (DPPIS) is a digital effort to solve farmers' crop problems quickly and effectively. It has been created by logically arranging the picture of various crop-based problems and the farmer will get help in crop production according to the amount of his land. By looking at the pictures here, the farmer can identify his problem himself and by clicking on the marked picture, the solution of the problem will appear on the monitor. Problems caused by field crops, pests, insects, fertilizer deficiencies or other causes;

Those problems and their solutions have been added. Multiple pictures of each problem and at least one representative picture have been added, so that the farmer can easily identify his problem. In order to help the farmers who are not able to know the problem of their crop well when they come to take agricultural advisory services, some of the common problems in the field are taken and computerized. Then the farmers are shown the pictures of those who do not come up with any problem. It turned out to be working very well. The farmer can identify the problem of his crop by looking at the pictures. Then the solution to the problem was added to the pictures. This time it will be helpful for both farmers and agricultural extension workers in solving crop problems. Multiple surveys were conducted to get the views of the farmers. It shows that 6% of the farmers who have not availed the service in this way and 100% of the farmers who have availed the service in this way think that the method is helpful for them. We think there will be an encouraging response. We have arranged numerous comments and suggestions from eminent persons.

The rationale for this initiative

Using the Agri Calculator will benefit both the service recipient and the service provider. The service recipient will get fast and accurate service and the service provider can easily and quickly solve any problem using the Agri Calculator. By using the Agri Calculator, the possible communication noise between the service recipient and the service provider can be brought down to the lowest level. The Agri Calculator actually helps the farmer to properly present his problem to the extension worker. Using it, an advanced farmer can solve his crop problems on his own.

t is farmer friendly: every picture used in the Agri Calculator is taken from the field and the picture is used exactly as the farmer sees the problem in the field. As a result, the farmer can recognize the problem correctly.
